About BMOS

Built for creators, hackers, and learners.

BMOS started as a custom Raspberry Pi command-line project focused on learning how operating systems, shells, packages, and customization work.

CLI Focused

BMOS provides a lightweight shell experience with commands, packages, and future online functionality.

Educational

Perfect for experimenting with Python, Linux, Raspberry Pi, and terminal environments.

Expandable

BMOS is built around future expansion through packages, updates, and repository systems.
