About BMOS

Built for developers.

BMOS is a lightweight terminal operating environment designed for experimentation, customization, learning, and creativity. Whether you are building projects, learning Linux, or experimenting with Raspberry Pi systems, BMOS gives you a minimal environment that stays out of your way.

Minimal by design

BMOS focuses on speed, simplicity, and clarity without unnecessary desktop clutter or background processes.

Built to expand

BMOS supports packages, repositories, updates, and future online features while remaining lightweight.

Made for experimentation

Perfect for Raspberry Pi projects, custom shells, learning Python, and exploring how operating systems work. 

BMOS Philosophy

• Fast startup
• Minimal interface
• Customizable shell
• Package support
• Raspberry Pi optimized
• Educational and expandable

BMOS is not trying to replace Linux.
It is built to help you explore it.
