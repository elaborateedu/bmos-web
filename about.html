About BMOS

Built for developers.

BMOS is a lightweight terminal operating environment made for experimentation, customization, learning, and building your own OS-like workflow.

Download BMOS Read Docs

Who we are

BMOS was founded by Jonathan Schoen, with the mission of making Linux CLIs more approachable & less scary. The redesigned command system is great for any skill level- whether a rookie to Linux or a skilled developer, BMOS is designed to be your partner the whole way through.

"The future is built by the people daring enough to expiriment it"
-Jonathan Schoen (Founder of BMOS)

What we do

Minimal

BMOS avoids unnecessary clutter and keeps the experience focused.

Expandable

BMOS can grow with packages, repositories, settings, and updates.

Experimental

BMOS is made for people who want to build, break, fix, and learn.

Backed by a Community

A great community that can help you every step of the way.
