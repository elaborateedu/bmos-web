About BMOS

Built for developers.

BMOS is a lightweight terminal operating environment made for experimentation, customization, learning, and building your own OS-like workflow.

What BMOS is.

BMOS is a custom command-line environment designed to be simple, expandable, and easy to understand.

Minimal

BMOS avoids unnecessary clutter and keeps the experience focused.

Expandable

BMOS can grow with packages, repositories, settings, and updates.

Experimental

BMOS is made for people who want to build, break, fix, and learn.
