Built for creators.

BMOS combines the flexibility of Linux-style systems with a clean shell environment designed for experimentation, customization, and lightweight workflows.

Minimal

BMOS removes unnecessary clutter and focuses on speed, simplicity, and usability.

Expandable

Install apps and utilities through packages and repositories to expand BMOS over time.

Educational

Learn command-line systems, shell environments, packages, updates, and system design concepts.

A system you can actually understand.

BMOS is intentionally lightweight and approachable. Instead of hiding how everything works, BMOS encourages exploration and modification.
