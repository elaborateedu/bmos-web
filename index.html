Quick Start

Get BMOS running in just a few minutes.

1. Download

Download the latest BMOS image from the downloads page.

2. Flash

Use Raspberry Pi Imager or Balena Etcher to flash BMOS onto a microSD card.

3. Boot

Insert the SD card into your Raspberry Pi and power on the system.
